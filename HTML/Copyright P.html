Copyright Protection for Local Creators

At Kikwetu Consortium, we believe in safeguarding the creativity and hard work of Kibra's talented artists, artisans, and creators. Our platform is designed to ensure that your intellectual property is respected and protected, empowering you to share your work with the world confidently.

Creative Showcase

We provide a space for local creators to showcase their unique works—whether it's art, fashion, music, or crafts. Each product or piece is registered on our platform, ensuring it’s properly attributed to you. When tourists or visitors purchase items or book experiences, you receive credit directly for your work, helping to prevent exploitation by third parties.

How to Protect Your Creations

  • Copyright Registration: Learn how to register your creative works officially to ensure legal protection.
  • Avoiding Copyright Infringement: Tips on how to spot and avoid having your work used without permission.
  • Licensing Your Work: Understand how to license your creations for commercial use and get compensated fairly.

Our Promise to Creators

We are committed to upholding the rights of local creators. Our platform offers an easy and secure way for you to share and sell your work, knowing that your intellectual property is protected. We actively monitor for any unauthorized use of your content and work with legal professionals to ensure that your rights are upheld.
